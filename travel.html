Travel

We know many of our guests will be attending from out-of-state, so we've included some tips for the best ways to navigate Chicago & Evanston travel!

Getting In

We recommend flying in to O'Hare International Airport.

Getting to Evanston

The fastest way to get between Evanston and O'Hare is by car; rideshare is available from Terminal 2 and car rentals are available from the O'Hare Multi-Modal Facility. If you chose to rent a car, we have been provided a 25% discount for all guests through Hertz.
For those who prefer public transport, you can take the Pulse Dempster Line bus from the O'Hare Multi-Modal Facility (Bay 9) to the Davis CTA Metra, which will place you right in the heart of downtown Evanston and within walking distance of both hotels.

Getting Around

Most of Evanston is walkable, and we encourage those who are able to explore the downtown area on foot. Chicago's iconic "El" is also an option - Evanston runs on the Purple line, which takes you from the Northernmost parts of the city all the way to the border of Evanston and Chicago. CTA and PACE buses are also available.
